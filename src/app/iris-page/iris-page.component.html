Iris

Iris is a podcasting company dedicated to providing high-quality, accessible, and affordable content for discerning listeners. To help them amplify their brand and reach a broader audience, I embarked on a project to develop a sophisticated, user-friendly website.

Role

UI designer

Project duration

2 weeks

Tools used

Figma

Project goal

My goal was to create an online presence that mirrors Iris’s commitment to courage, innovation, and freshness. The new website features a striking landing page in their signature red, a compelling “Our Values” section, and an engaging About Page that tells the story of Iris and their team. It also includes detailed product pages for each podcast, enhanced with high-quality visuals and multimedia, and a blog section for sharing insightful podcasting content. The design emphasizes luxury and functionality, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices while reinforcing our brand identity.
